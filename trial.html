THE ALMONDS is a visionary masterpiece nestled in the heart of SOUTH C, the new lifestyle hub of Nairobi. The project boasts a selection of 3 & 4-bedroom residences. Launched in December 2021, The project was completed and handed over to new homeowners in August 2023. The project took exactly 20 Months to complete.

Exhibiting the true essence of luxury living is this magnificent, ultra-modern piece of architectural mastery in South C off Ole Shapara Avenue. Anchored within the exquisite quiet surroundings of South C, The Almonds comprises uniquely designed 3&4 Bedroom apartments carefully synchronized to encapsulate luxury living.
